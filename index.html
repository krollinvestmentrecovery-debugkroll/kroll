Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I withdraw my funds?

Navigate to the dashboard, enter your banking details, and click "Request Withdrawal". A fee & tax will be applied.

What is the Take Profit requirement?

You need to reach 99% Take Profit to unlock withdrawal options. The progress bar in your dashboard shows your current TP status.

How long does withdrawal processing take?

Once payment confirmation is received, funds are released within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification when your withdrawal is complete.

Is my information secure?

Yes. Kroll uses 256-bit SSL encryption, secure servers, and follows strict data protection protocols. Your information is never shared with third parties.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and cryptocurrency payments. All payments go through our secure payment gateway.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us via live chat (bottom right), WhatsApp, email at support@kroll-recovery.com, or call +27 731564265.
